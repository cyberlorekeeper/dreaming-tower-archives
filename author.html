Meet the Cyber Lorekeeper

I’m Victoria Mosby — a storyteller, cybersecurity professional, and keeper of these chronicles. The Dreaming Tower and the Digital Realm are my way of translating complex threats, resilience, and human choices into legends you can feel.

By day, I guide teams through digital risk. By night, I’m weaving field notes, characters, and dispatches that blur the line between security research and speculative fiction. Welcome to the archive where those worlds converge.
