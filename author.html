Lore + Security

Short Bio

By day, I analyze complex threat chains, fractured supply routes, and the way people, process, and code collide. By night, I render those patterns into Cipheria’s sagas so readers can explore the consequences with curiosity instead of fear. My goal is to make security human, memorable, and a little wondrous.

When I’m not tracing incidents, you’ll find me drafting new chapters, sketching city maps for the Dreaming Tower archives, or teaching teams how to spot the early whispers of a breach.
