Welcome to the Known World

The world is vast and divided, shaped by four great continents separated by treacherous seas. Order exists, but only because it is constantly defended. Civilization here survives through vigilance, memory, and cooperation in the face of chaos.

This is a realm where castle walls are reinforced with encrypted wards, where threats take physical form, and where magic and technology have blurred into the same relentless discipline: protecting what endures.

The Four Continents

Each continent carries its own balance of power, culture, and danger. Together they form the Known World, connected by trade, politics, and the shared struggle to keep chaos from taking root.

Valorath, The Eastern Continent

The most interconnected and politically stable of the known continents, Valorath is shaped by established kingdoms, trade routes, and carefully maintained alliances. Stability here is negotiated, defended, and paid for in compromise.

On its eastern shores stands Cipheria, a regional stabilizer known for coordinated defenses and a refusal to ignore warning signs.

Sundara, The Southern Continent

Warm, resource-rich, and culturally diverse, Sundara thrives through independent city-states and merchant leagues. Its influence travels north through rare materials, exotic goods, and traditions that do not bend easily to foreign rule.

Narvoss, The Northern Continent

Cold, harsh, and fiercely insular, Narvoss forges resilient peoples and formidable defenses. Its kingdoms open their borders rarely, and when they do, it is on their terms. Legends speak of northern fortifications that do not fail, even when the world around them does.

Khal-Teryn, The Western Continent

Separated by perilous seas, Khal-Teryn remains a fragmented rumor to eastern scholars. Contact is rare, artifacts are priceless, and every confirmed detail only deepens the unsettling suspicion that the west remembers a history the rest of the world has misplaced.

The Kingdom of Cipheria

On Valorath’s eastern coast lies Cipheria, a resilient power known less for conquest and more for survival. It endures through coordination, preparedness, and a stubborn refusal to ignore warning signs.

Cipheria is governed through collaboration, balancing defense, economics, intelligence, and historical knowledge. Its strength is not a single weapon or ruler, but a system designed to respond before crises become catastrophes.

At the center of this system stands the Dreaming Tower. Once a simple observatory, it has become a living archive and early warning beacon. The Tower watches, remembers, and whispers what history insists we learn.

The Crown City anchors the realm, supported by vital ports, fortified borders, and a network of settlements built to hold the line when the wild lands press too close.
