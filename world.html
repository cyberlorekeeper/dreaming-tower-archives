Welcome to the Known World

The world is vast and divided, shaped by four great continents separated by treacherous seas. Order exists, but only because it is constantly defended. Civilization here survives through vigilance, memory, and cooperation in the face of chaos.

This is a realm where castle walls are reinforced with encrypted wards, where threats take physical form, and where magic and technology have blurred into the same relentless discipline: protecting what endures.

The Kingdom of Cipheria

On Valorath’s eastern coast lies Cipheria, our primary focus and the setting for the tales preserved in these archives.

A Nation Built on Vigilance

Cipheria is not the largest kingdom, but it is among the most secure and well-organized. A collaborative Council balances military strength, prosperity, intelligence, historical wisdom, and royal authority.

At the heart of this success stands the Dreaming Tower: ancient, semi-sentient, and watching. It began as an Observatory and became something far greater: a living repository, warning system, and symbol of learning from the past.

Threats from the Wilds and Beyond

Civilization is not the default state. Beyond the borders lie untamed territories where threats take physical form. These Glitchborne are born from corruption, chaos, and the breakdown of order.

Some emerge from wilderness. Others slip through rifts from the Space Besides. And some are created by civilization itself: corrupted experiments, mistakes, and weaponized dangers that blur the line between external threat and internal failure.

The Space Besides

Reality is not as solid as it appears. Between the stable world exists a liminal realm of distortion and wrongness: not another dimension, but the gaps in reality itself.

When reality cracks

Rifts appear most often in wild lands, ancient disaster sites, and places weakened by prolonged magical use or catastrophic events. The Fractured Waters are the most persistent example, but smaller rifts can open anywhere, without warning.

Understanding and sealing these breaches remains one of Cipheria’s ongoing challenges, requiring both the Tower’s ancient knowledge and the Council’s coordinated response.

The Isle of 404: Myth or Reality?

Ask any scholar, and you’ll receive a different answer. Some insist it is myth. Others point to centuries of sightings, recorded by witnesses who swear they walked its shores before it vanished.

The prevailing theory is stranger than both: the Isle of 404 slips between realities, spending time in the normal world before phasing into the Space Besides, only to emerge elsewhere when it returns.

Those who land there risk being stranded when it phases away. The scattered inhabitants, drawn from many eras and cultures, have built a fragile society on an island that may abandon them at any moment.

No one has mapped a reliable pattern. It remains lost, wandering, found only by accident, fate, or perhaps by those who carry fragments of impossible knowledge in their broken memories.

A World of Stories

Kingdoms rise and fall. Trade routes shift. Threats evolve. And through it all, the Dreaming Tower watches, records, and remembers.

These archives preserve the stories that matter: crises and response, decisions made under impossible pressure, and individuals who stand against the tide of chaos.

The Tower dreams. The Kingdom endures. And the stories continue.
