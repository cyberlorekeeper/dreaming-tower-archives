Welcome to the Known World

The world is vast and divided, shaped by four great continents separated by treacherous seas. Order exists, but only because it is constantly defended. Civilization here survives through vigilance, memory, and cooperation in the face of chaos.

This is a realm where castle walls are reinforced with encrypted wards, where threats take physical form, and where magic and technology have blurred into the same relentless discipline: protecting what endures.

The Four Continents

Four landmasses shape the balance of power, trade, and threat. Valorath holds the most stable network of kingdoms. Sundara thrives on trade and tradition. Narvoss endures through defense. Khal-Teryn remains an unsettled question across hostile seas.

Valorath | The Eastern Continent

The most politically stable and interconnected of the known continents. Established kingdoms, trade networks, and layered infrastructure create a delicate balance of cooperation and rivalry. Cipheria rises along the eastern coast as a beacon of organized security and governance.

Sundara | The Southern Continent

Warm, resource-rich, and culturally diverse. Merchant republics, city-states, and roaming nations trade rare materials and unique traditions northward. Influence here is measured in routes, pacts, and leverage more than crowns.

Narvoss | The Northern Continent

Harsh and fiercely independent. Narvoss is known for isolationist kingdoms, legendary defenses, and the occasional sophisticated threat that emerges from its deep cold.

Khal-Teryn | The Western Continent

Shrouded in mystery across treacherous seas. Ancient civilizations, unfamiliar traditions, and rare emissaries. Artifacts fetch extraordinary prices and raise more questions than answers.

The Seas Between

The oceans bind the world together and keep it apart. Routes exist, but certainty does not.

The Uncharted Seas

Vast expanses remain poorly mapped. Established trade routes exist, but much lies unexplored. The ocean keeps its secrets, and sometimes it keeps ships.

The Fractured Waters

In places, reality grows thin. Rifts open without warning, spilling strange creatures and corrupting influence into the mortal world. Most captains detour if they can.

The Wandering Tracks

Ancient sightings of the Isle of 404 marked with question marks and dotted lines. Centuries of contradictory reports, and the persistent hope the island can be found on purpose.

The Kingdom of Cipheria

On Valorath’s eastern coast lies Cipheria, our primary focus and the home of the Dreaming Tower. It is not the largest kingdom, but it is among the most vigilant.

A Nation Built on Vigilance

Governed by a collaborative Council balancing defense, prosperity, intelligence, history, and authority, Cipheria survived crises that shattered others.

The Dreaming Tower stands at the center: ancient, semi-sentient, and relentlessly observant. It archives what was, warns of what is, and helps prepare for what is coming.

Cities and Regions

  • Crown City: capital built in rings of increasing security.
  • Harbordeep and Tide’s Gate: port cities where trade and infrastructure converge.
  • Ironhearth: industrial footholds forging tools of defense.
  • Brighthollow: agricultural heartlands feeding the realm.
  • The Borderlands: frontier towns watching the wilds.

The Space Besides

Reality is not as solid as it appears. Between stable places lies a liminal realm of distortion, cracks, and wrongness. Most of the time, those cracks remain sealed.

But sometimes, reality breaks. When it does, creatures slip through into the mortal world, bringing corruption and chaos with them. Rifts appear most often in wild lands, disaster sites, and places weakened by prolonged magical use or catastrophic events.

Understanding and sealing these rifts is one of Cipheria’s ongoing burdens, requiring the Dreaming Tower’s knowledge and coordinated response from the Council.

Teaser

Rifts do not just open. They are invited by negligence, fed by shortcuts, and widened by panic.

The Isle of 404: Myth or Reality?

Ask ten scholars about the Isle of 404 and you will get ten different answers. Myth, hoax, cursed land, or something stranger that slips between worlds.

What the Records Suggest

The Isle appears to phase between the known world and the Space Besides. Those who land there risk being stranded when it shifts away. Inhabitants from different eras and cultures have built a desperate society on a shore that may vanish at any moment.

No one has determined the pattern. No map can track it reliably. Found by accident, fate, or perhaps by those carrying fragments of impossible knowledge.

The Tower dreams. The Kingdom endures. And the stories continue.
