From the Lorekeeper’s Journal

I write these passages for the one who will follow after me.

This chronicle will be the archive of all I have observed, deciphered, and endured — a record of the forces that shape our realm and the secrets passed down by Lorekeepers long before my time.

Within these entries you will find lessons carved from my wanderings through the Dreaming Tower, field notes from the unstable frontier of Glitchborne, and truths I can no longer keep to myself. May these writings guide you where I once walked alone.

The first public excerpts from these archives are coming soon — shared as tales, newsletters, and recovered pages from the Lorekeeper’s desk.

No spam. Just dispatches from the Dreaming Tower when new chapters awaken.
