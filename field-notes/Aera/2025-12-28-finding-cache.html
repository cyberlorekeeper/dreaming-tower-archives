AERA'S LOG: Entry 7 Date: 14th of Harvest Moon, Year of the Fracture-Mended Location: Aboard the Persistent Query, three days from last confirmed Isle sighting Cache woke me at dawn insisting he "remembered something important" about navigation. It turned out to be a fragment of a sea shanty he couldn't finish and directions to a port that doesn't exist anymore. Or maybe never existed. With his memory, it's impossible to tell. "I'm trying," he snapped when I couldn't hide my frustration. The shimmer around him pulsed indigo—his version of hurt feelings. I apologized. It's not his fault his memories are corrupted. And honestly, without him, I'd have no leads at all. The Remembral shows me my sister's face, her voice, the way she'd tuck her hair behind her ear when concentrating on a difficult transcription. But it doesn't show me how to find her. Cache insists the Isle will appear "when the tides align with absence." I have no idea what that means. Neither does he, really.
